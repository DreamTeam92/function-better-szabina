Refine the entire website to create a truly premium, luxury experience.

Do not change the structure or content hierarchy. Instead, improve the visual design, spacing, typography and user experience throughout.

The website should feel closer to a luxury wellness clinic, boutique private coaching brand or high-end healthcare practice than a fitness website.

GENERAL DESIGN

• Increase white space throughout the website.
• Create a more spacious, elegant and sophisticated layout.
• Improve the visual hierarchy using typography rather than decorative elements.
• Use generous margins and consistent vertical spacing between sections.
• Create a calm, luxurious browsing experience.

TYPOGRAPHY

• Use elegant, modern typography.
• Increase the size of section headings.
• Improve line spacing and readability.
• Use lighter font weights where appropriate.
• Ensure headings feel refined and editorial.

COLOUR PALETTE

Keep the palette soft and timeless.

Primary colours:
• White
• Warm off-white
• Soft beige
• Charcoal
• Subtle black

Use gold only as a very subtle accent.

Avoid bright colours or anything that feels commercial.

HERO SECTION

Create a stronger first impression.

• Make the hero section full screen.
• Increase the size of my professional photograph.
• Give the image more breathing room.
• Keep the text aligned elegantly beside the portrait.
• Add a subtle fade-in animation when the page loads.
• Make the CTA button elegant rather than bold or flashy.

SECTIONS

Increase spacing between every section.

Each section should feel like an individual chapter with room to breathe.

Avoid crowded layouts.

CARDS

Refine every card throughout the website.

• Softer shadows
• Rounded corners
• More padding
• Elegant hover animations
• Smooth transitions
• Premium appearance

SERVICES

Avoid making all service cards feel identical.

Introduce subtle visual variation while keeping the design consistent.

The section should resemble an editorial luxury website rather than a standard grid.

MEET SABINA

Transform this section into a premium personal profile.

Create a balanced layout that highlights:

• My portrait
• My experience
• My qualifications
• Statistics
• My personal philosophy

The section should establish trust and authority immediately.

CLIENT EXPERIENCE

Present testimonials in a more premium way.

Use elegant cards or a smooth carousel.

Highlight five-star reviews beautifully.

Make social proof feel authentic and luxurious.

LOCATION

Give the map more space.

Clearly communicate that I provide private sessions in clients' homes and luxury villas across Costa Blanca South.

ANIMATIONS

Use subtle premium animations only.

Examples:

• Fade in
• Gentle slide up
• Smooth hover effects
• Elegant button transitions

Animations should enhance the experience without becoming distracting.

MOBILE EXPERIENCE

Ensure the website feels just as luxurious on mobile.

Maintain generous spacing.

Avoid cramped layouts.

FINAL GOAL

The finished website should look like it was designed by a premium web design agency for an exclusive private movement specialist.

Every design decision should communicate professionalism, trust, expertise, calmness and luxury.
